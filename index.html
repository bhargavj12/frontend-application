Our timeline

Get grasp of our milestones

  • 2016

    100,000 trees planted

    one hundred thousand trees were planted in a field across from Starkey’s Loop, a hiking trail just outside of Guelph, Ontario, Canada. The trees were planted by former and current students of the Community Environmental Leadership Program (CELP), in the midst of CELP founder, teacher, and life-long Mike Elrick’s battle with global warming. One hundred Thousand Trees is a response to the legacy he has left to so many.
